Vincent's Jumprope Calculator

Press "Calculate Grade" to calculate

How to use:

  1. Open your jumprope grades
  2. Choose a PI to calculate
  3. Enter the assignment grades (top is oldest assignment, bottom is newest
  4. Press "Add New" to add a new assignment or Calculate Grade to estimate your grade

Notes:

  • If a field is invalid (blank, containing text, or above 4, it will be ignored in the calculation.
  • Refreshing the page will clear all progress
  • Pressing "Add new" will automatically calculate the score with what you put in so far
